Who am I?

I'm Nikhil Chandra Madanapalli, a strong believer of the fact that data is more than just numbers! And if used correctly, it has a large potential to make a difference at an organisation. My passion to influence decision making at a sporting organisation using data-driven insights only keeps growing. I enjoy making sports analysis content and publishing them on Twitter and Medium every now and then. Below are examples of my work. Sequences Dashboard - Lollipop Chart
